Triada Calendar

Three months at a glance — right from your menu bar.

Triada Calendar is a fast, distraction‑free calendar for macOS. View past, current, and next month together, with instant month/year navigation and automatic Light/Dark Mode.

Why it’s different

Most menu bar calendars show only one or two months. Triada shows three with a clean, native layout.

Privacy‑first

No accounts. No analytics. Works fully offline.

Built for focus

Opens instantly without switching apps — perfect for planning dates while you work.
