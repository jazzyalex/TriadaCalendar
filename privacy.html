Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 12, 2025

Triada Calendar is a lightweight macOS menu bar app that shows three months at a glance. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we ("we", "us", or "our") collect and how we handle it.

Summary

  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.
  • No analytics, no advertising, no third‑party SDKs.
  • The app works entirely offline and does not transmit data to our servers.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any information from you or your device. The app does not require an account, does not access contacts, calendars, location, camera, microphone, or photos, and does not read or transmit any content.

Device Permissions

Triada Calendar does not request device permissions.

Third‑Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share data with third parties. The app does not include third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking.

Children’s Privacy

While Triada Calendar is rated 4+ and suitable for general audiences, it is not directed to children. Since we do not collect data, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Security

Because the app does not collect or transmit data, no personal information is stored on our servers. Standard platform security provided by Apple applies to installation and updates via the Mac App Store.

International Transfers

No personal data is collected or transferred.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if app functionality changes. If future versions add features such as calendar integration, we will update this page to describe any data handled by the app and your choices.

Contact
